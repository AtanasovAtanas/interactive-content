## Description
Write a program, which **prints all unique pairs** in an array of integers whose **sum is equal to a given number**.

### Example
| **Input** | **Output** |
| --- | --- |
| 1 7 6 2 19 23 | 1 7 |
| 8 | 6 2 |

### Example
| **Input** | **Output** |
| --- | --- |
| 14 20 60 13 7 19 8 | 14 13 |
| 27 | 20 7 |
| | 19 8 |
