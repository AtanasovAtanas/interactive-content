## Description
Write a program, which prints **common elements** in **two arrays**.

You have to **compare the elements** of the **second array** to the elements of the **first**.

### Example
| **Input** | **Output** |
| --- | --- |
| Hey hello 2 4 | 4 hello |
| 10 hey 4 hello | |
