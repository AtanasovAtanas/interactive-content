## Description

Write a program to **display numbers** from given start to given end and their sum. 

All the numbers will be **integers**. 

On the **first line** you will receive the **start** number, on the **second** the **end** number.

## Example
### Input
5

10
### Output
5 6 7 8 9 10

Sum: 45
